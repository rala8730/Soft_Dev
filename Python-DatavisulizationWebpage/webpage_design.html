Carbon Emissions In Each State

This map shows the amount of carbon emissions in for each state in Million metric tons of carbon dioxide. You can find find and compare the data for years 1990, 2000, 2010, and 2013 which is most recent data available. The source for this data is U.S Energy Information Administration (EIA).
Total Carbon emissions for each state is the addition of emissions caused by Coal, Petroleum Products and Natural Gas. You can find the data details for each state by moving the curor on the desired state in the map
